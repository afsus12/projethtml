What is Lambo

Automobili Lamborghini SpA ( Italian : Automobili Lamborghini SpA ), generally referred to as Lamborghini, is an Italian automobile manufacturer . The company was founded by industrial magnate Ferruccio Lamborghini in 1963, with the aim of producing a car for the grand tour and competing with existing offerings from other manufacturers such as Ferrari .
